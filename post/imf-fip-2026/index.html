Starting the IMF Fund Internship Program (FIP) this summer

Jun 1, 2026 · 1 min read

I’ll be joining the International Monetary Fund this summer as part of the Fund Internship Program (FIP), from June through August 2026.

During the internship, I’ll be working on a research question studying how differing demographic trends across countries shape the equilibrium quantity and price of safe assets globally — and, in turn, the level and dynamics of the global equity premium.
